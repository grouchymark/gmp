Legal Video Depositions

The late Tony Vehar, Esq., first introduced and hired me to film legal video depositions. For obvious reasons, I cannot include samples of my production in this realm, though, there's a wide array of demos of my work as a videographer and video eding and production elsewhere on this site and affiliated social media presences.

I utilize a pair of JVC HD video-cameras and a separate H2 Zoom Audio Recorder, if necessary, and have filmed in court rooms, law offices, doctor's offices and more. I am certainly willing to travel and can be available for multi-day depositions. At present, I can generally free my schedule at a moment's notice.

I believe my chief advantages are affordability and quick turnaround. My rates, depending on the circumstances, begin at $50 per hour and I offer same day/next day delivery of DVD production of the filmed legal video depositions.
