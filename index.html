About Grouchy Mark Productions

This web presence will detail and showcase the expanding array of services I strive to deliver. 


Some are locally/regionally based, such as Legal Video Depositions or being booked as a MobileDJ/Emcee for a variety of events, but the vast majority are available most anywhere in the world. 





This site remake has been relegated to the back burner, as other business dealings took precedence, but hopefully, fresh content highlighting business and promotional aims will soon be updated, in comprehensive fashion.

.

I opt for the definition of "updated" rather than completed, believing websites are comprised of dynamic and evolving elements, meaning they're never really in a state of 100% completeness.


Thank you so much for finding your way here!
